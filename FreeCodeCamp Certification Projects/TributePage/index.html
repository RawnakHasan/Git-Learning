Albert Einstein

Theoretical Physicist

Here is a time line of Albert Einstein:

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, Kingdom of W rttemberg, German Empire
  • 1894 - Moved to Switzerland to attend the Swiss Federal Polytechnic University
  • 1905 - Completed his thesis and was awarded a Ph.D. by the University of Zurich
  • 1915 - Developed the theory of general relativity
  • 1919 - Predicted the existence of gravitational waves
  • 1921 - Awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics for his explanation of the photoelectric effect
  • 1940 - Moved to the United States and became a U.S. citizen in 1940
  • 1941 - Received the Nobel Prize in Physics for his explanation of the photoelectric effect
  • 1955 - Died on April 18, 1955, in Princeton, New Jersey, USA
